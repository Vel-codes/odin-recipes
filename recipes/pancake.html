Pancakes Recipe

Description

Fluffy and golden pancakes made from simple ingredients like flour, milk, and eggs.

Perfect for a cozy breakfast. Serve with syrup, fruits, or your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Mix flour, milk, and eggs in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour a ladle of batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form.
  4. Flip and cook the other side until golden.
  5. Serve hot with syrup or toppings of choice.
