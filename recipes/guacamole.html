Guacamole Recipe

Description

Fresh and creamy guacamole made from ripe avocados, lime, and spices.

Perfect as a dip or topping for tacos, nachos, and sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Lime
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh.
  2. Mash the avocado in a bowl until creamy.
  3. Add lime juice and a pinch of salt.
  4. Mix well and adjust seasoning to taste.
  5. Serve fresh as a dip or topping.
