Lasagna Recipe

Description

This easy lasagna combines tender noodles, creamy ricotta, and rich

marinara sauce. Perfect for a quick and comforting meal that everyone will enjoy.

40–45 minutes until bubbly and golden. Let it rest a few minutes before serving.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Marinara sauce

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles until tender.
  2. Spread a layer of marinara sauce in a baking dish.
  3. Add a layer of noodles, then ricotta, then sauce. Repeat layers.
  4. Top with sauce and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30–40 minutes.
  5. Let it rest a few minutes, then serve.
